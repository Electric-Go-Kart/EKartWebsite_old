    • Abstract:

    The purpose of our project is to continue the development of last year's Electric Go-Kart. Our project is closely connected with the ECE Outreach Team, with the main purpose of the go-kart being educating students about engineering concepts.

    Our core goals this year are focused on increasing the dimensions of electrical and computer engineering covered in the kart. By redesigning our controls platform to enable more sensors and compute power, we plan to offer a wealth of ECE application demos for student enjoyment.

    Background:

    Last year's team presented us with a fully-functional electric go-kart. The kart features a battery system with two electric motors, a custom chassis, and an embedded controls system for data readout. Feel free to check out current pictures of the kart and it's progress on the

    Design Goals:

    • Redesign controls platform
    • Redesign dashboard display with improved graphics
    • Autonomous braking with detection and ranging sensors
    • Solar charging for the kart's battery

    Project Constraints:

    • Lack of mechanical engineering students means no major mechanical improvements are possible
