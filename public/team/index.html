    • Electrical Engineering:

    Vani Kapoor

    Vani is an Electrical Engineering student graduating in Spring 2022. She is interested in Control systems within renewable energy and vehicle automation. She joined the Go-Kart team to combine her passion for cars and engineering while being able to learn more about different aspects of how electric vehicles work. She is acting as Project Manager as well as working as a systems engineer for the control and power systems of the project. After graduation, Vani wishes to pursue her interests in control system design, renewable energy, and systems engineering.

    Nikola Durand

    Nikola Durand is a Senior at Colorado State University studying electrical engineering. With almost five years of motorsport and vehicle design experience, he has played critical roles in multiple generations of cars across 3 teams. During the 2021-2022 academic year, Nikola is working with the engineering department building a small electric vehicle, focusing on systems engineering and integration from different vehicle teams. In his free time, Nikola enjoys cryptography and working on vehicle modifications. After graduation, he is pursuing a career in vehicle engineering and design.

    Rico Barela

    Rico Barela is an Electrical Engineer student graduating in Spring of 2022. He joined the Go-Kart team because he has always had a passion for working on vehicles starting at a young age, and is building his own Go-Kart that is mostly focused on performance. He is interested in power engineering and mechanical concepts. His main focus for the project is the power system, involving the power delivery to the Kart and possible solar panel charging. After graduation he plans to get into the car industry and design/build electric motors.

    David Neitenbach

    David is an Electrical Engineering Student Graduation in Spring of 2022. He is interested in power engineering with a focus in solar power. He joined the Go Kart team because of his interest in cars; especially electric ones. He is focusing on power for the project from solar panels to power delivery to the motors. After Graduation he hopes to work in the power industry and help it move to cleaner energy.

    Computer Engineering:

    Ryan Guidice

    Ryan is a Computer Engineering student graduating in the spring of 2022. He joined the Go-Kart team because it sounded like a great combination of mechanical, electrical and software principles, with ample opportunity to contribute in all areas. He is working on the software and controls side of the project. His interests post-graduation include VLSI design and verification, FPGA development, and embedded programming.
